Freedom Fighters

Mahatma Gandhi

Date of Birth: October 2, 1869

Date of Passing: January 30, 1948

Role in Freedom Struggle: Leader of the Indian National Congress, pioneer of nonviolent resistance, and advocate for civil rights.

Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel

Date of Birth: October 31, 1875

Date of Passing: December 15, 1950

Role in Freedom Struggle: Leader in the Indian National Congress and instrumental in the integration of princely states into independent India.

Tantia Tope

Date of Birth: 1814

Date of Passing: April 18, 1859

Role in Freedom Struggle: Indian rebel leader during the Indian Rebellion of 1857 against British rule.

Lal Bahadur Shastri

Date of Birth: October 2, 1904

Date of Passing: January 11, 1966

Role in Freedom Struggle: Prominent leader in the Indian independence movement and the second Prime Minister of India.

Subhas Chandra Bose

Date of Birth: January 23, 1897

Date of Passing: August 18, 1945

Role in Freedom Struggle: Leader of the Indian National Army (INA) and known for his slogan "Give me blood, and I shall give you freedom."

Jawaharlal Nehru

Date of Birth: November 14, 1889

Date of Passing: May 27, 1964

Role in Freedom Struggle: First Prime Minister of India and a key figure in the Indian independence movement.
